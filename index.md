Benefits
========
Data Migration
--------------
We provide complete data migration from your existing system to G Suite without any downtime. We migrate all the emails, calendar events and contacts for your entire organization.

On-site User Training
---------------------
We get your users up and running with G Suite with on-site training that covers the essentials plus tips & tricks for various G Suite apps including Gmail, Calendar and Hangouts. Additionally, we train your users how to work and collaborate using Google Drive.

G Suite Admin Training
----------------------
Your IT personnel get a comprehensive overview of all G Suite features and the administrative tasks available to them across the entire G Suite which include user management, mobile device management, security and many more. Furthermore, we can provide training for using Google Apps Script which provides powerful programmatic access to most of G Suite's admin tasks.

Active DNS Monitoring
---------------------
We keep an eye on you domain names and their DNS configuration and alert you immediately if any change that might affect emails in your organization. We also alert you when your domain is about to expire.
